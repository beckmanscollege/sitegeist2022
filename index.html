Build a bridge between the present and the future by writing a digital letter to your future self (or someone else)! Simply fill in your name (or an alias) and the email-adress of your intended recipient, write your letter and the message will arrive ten years from now in the recipients inbox.
* Your message will be partially handled by a human fyi. Maybe you’d like to send the digital postman a message directly, to which they would happily reply to in 10 years time. If not, your message will simply arrive to its intended recipient 10 years from now.
